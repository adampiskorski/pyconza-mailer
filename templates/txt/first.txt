PyConZA 2023 returns to Durban!
========================
PyConZA 2023 will be in Durban on 5 - 6 October 2023.
It is a hybrid conference, with the in-person portion again held at the Premier Splendid Inn at uMhlanga, and the online part hosted on Discord.

Tickets
=====
You can buy tickets right now at: https://za.pycon.org/tickets/buy-tickets/
We have different rates depending on your situation and needs.  You can find out about these different rates here: https://za.pycon.org/tickets/ticket-prices/

Talks
====
We are still looking for and accepting talks from both remote and in-person speakers.
If you’d like to speak on any Python-related topic, we’d love to hear from you.
See how to submit talks at: https://za.pycon.org/talks/how-to-submit-a-talk/
