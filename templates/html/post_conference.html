Next, PyConZA in Cape Town
We have decided to host PyConZA 2024 in Cape Town and we're looking for organisers to help us out. While we need local organisers to fill certain roles on the ground, there are also many tasks which can be done remotely.
We welcome volunteers from anywhere! Please contact us via email at team@za.pycon.org, or reach out to anyone with the #organiser tag on our Discord.
We will announce the exact venue early next year, once we have scouted out the options.
